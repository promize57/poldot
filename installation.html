INSTALLATION

Installation is a pivotal phase within EPCIC (Engineering, Procurement, Construction, Installation, and Commissioning) projects in the oil and gas industry, encompassing the physical deployment and commissioning of all project components.

We specialize in key procurement categories, including:

  • Topside structures, jackets, and metallic caissons
  • Bridges, decks, and helidecks
  • Suction & driven piles, boat bumpers, protectors, and riser guards
  • Pipe spooling, flare/vent booms, equipment modules, and skids
  • Subsea manifolds, PLEMS (Production, Loading, Export, Manifold Systems), and FLEMS (Flowline, Export, Manifold Systems)

Our installation services span the entire process, from meticulous initial planning and preparation through to final commissioning and ongoing maintenance. Our team comprises highly skilled and experienced professionals, certified in the latest installation techniques and technologies, ensuring safe and efficient project execution.

We adopt a systematic approach, beginning with thorough planning and preparation. This involves identifying and ensuring the availability of all necessary resources—equipment, materials, and personnel—in optimal working condition. Crucially, we conduct comprehensive site assessments to identify and mitigate potential hazards or constraints, including detailed analysis of environmental factors like weather and sea state.

During the installation phase, we collaborate closely with clients and stakeholders to ensure projects are completed punctually and within budget. We leverage cutting-edge installation technologies and equipment to maximize safety and efficiency, complemented by regular quality control inspections to guarantee adherence to required standards. Our robust tracking system provides real-time oversight, enabling us to promptly identify and resolve any emerging issues.

Upon completion of installation, we conduct a final commissioning process to verify correct operation and compliance with all standards. We also provide essential training to client personnel on how to effectively operate and maintain the newly installed facilities, contributing to the project's long-term longevity and sustainability.

Overall, our installation services are meticulously designed to ensure client projects are completed safely, efficiently, and on schedule. Our team of experienced professionals, combined with our strategic partnerships, empowers us to deliver high-quality, fully integrated solutions that consistently exceed expectations. We are dedicated to providing superior service, ensuring a smooth and efficient installation process, and prioritizing safety, quality, and timely completion at every stage.
