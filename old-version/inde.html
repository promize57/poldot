WE ARE POLDOT

Poldot Oil and Gas is a wholly Nigerian company, fully compliant with Nigerian laws, and dedicated to fostering local content development and fulfilling our corporate social responsibilities.

We offer a comprehensive suite of EPCIC services, encompassing engineering design, concept development, procurement, EPC/turnkey solutions, construction, consulting, commissioning, operations, and maintenance.

Our team of highly skilled and agile professional engineers, consultants, and project managers is poised to provide exceptional service for your oil and gas EPCIC needs.
