ENGINEERING

Our engineering and design services are a cornerstone of successful EPCIC (Engineering, Procurement, Construction, Installation, and Commissioning) projects within the oil and gas sector. We offer expertise across all critical disciplines, including chemical and process, mechanical, electrical, control and instrumentation, fire, structural, civil, cathodic protection, architectural, and industrial engineering. This comprehensive capability ensures we can manage every project aspect, from initial concept to final commissioning.

In the foundational stages, we conduct conceptual designs and thorough feasibility studies. These are vital for helping clients assess project viability, identify potential challenges, and pinpoint the most cost-effective and efficient solutions. Our analysis extends to technical and commercial considerations, such as resource availability, labor and material costs, and environmental/community impact, enabling us to proactively identify and mitigate risks.

Following initial studies, we advance to the Pre-FEED and Front-End Engineering Design (FEED) stages. Here, we develop more granular designs and engineering plans, integrating all technical and commercial insights. This includes producing essential documents like process flow diagrams and piping and instrumentation diagrams, all while rigorously adhering to safety and environmental regulations.

The detailed engineering phase involves creating precise construction and installation plans. This encompasses comprehensive engineering specifications, detailed drawings, and bills of materials. We maintain close collaboration with clients and stakeholders through regular meetings and reviews, ensuring all project aspects align with requirements, stay on track, and remain within budget.

During the project's execution, we provide crucial technical support, including on-site supervision, stringent quality control and assurance, and commissioning assistance. Our commitment extends to operational support, offering troubleshooting and maintenance guidance to ensure facilities operate at peak efficiency and safety.

Our end-to-end engineering and design services are dedicated to ensuring client projects are completed on time and within budget. Our diverse team of experienced engineers works collaboratively with clients, translating their needs into effective solutions. We are committed to delivering high-quality, cost-effective outcomes that consistently exceed expectations and uphold the highest project standards.
