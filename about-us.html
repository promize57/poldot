About Poldot Oil and Gas

Who We Are

We’re Poldot Oil and Gas Limited— a team of everyday Nigerians who started out on the consumption side of the energy story through our tech and data centre venture, PoldotMining. Realizing the scale of our own energy use, we knew it was time to do more than just consume — it was time to give back.

Backed by a group of experienced professionals, we created Poldot Oil and Gas to make clean, affordable gas energy available to others, deliver comprehensive EPC services, and champion safer, smarter, and more sustainable ways to power Nigeria forward.

From CNG, LPG, and LNG supply to transport, storage, procurement, and EPC execution, we’re here to fuel the future. Future Energy, Today!

Our Mission

To deliver clean and reliable gas energy solutions, while providing world-class EPC services to the heart of Nigeria’s gas industry — all with safety as our daily habit, not just a policy.

Our Vision

To become Nigeria’s most trusted gas brand and a leading EPC services provider — known for putting safety first, embracing innovation, and making clean energy the easy choice for every Nigerian.

Core Values

  • Safety Always – We protect our people, our customers, and the environment like family.
  • Integrity – We are transparent, accountable, and stand by our word.
  • Smart Thinking – Innovation drives how we work and how we grow.
  • Care for the Planet – Clean energy isn’t just what we do — it’s what we believe in.
  • Top Quality – Whether it’s cylinders, EPC services, or customer care — we never cut corners.
  • Respect – We create a positive, inclusive space where everyone is valued.
  • Team Spirit – We win together, grow together, and build stronger communities together.
